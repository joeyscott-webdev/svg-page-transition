About Joey Scott & FETEMP

Joey is the creator of Frontend Templates and the instructor in the tutorials.

After a 12 year career in a field he didnt enjoy, Joey decided to take a leap and do what he loved and became a web developer. After successfully transitioning careers in a short period of time Joey chose to start making tutorials for others who are exploring web development as a hobby or career. Frontend Templates is the result of that decision and were happy your here!

Joey is also a family oriented father of two children and enjoys many nerdy things which he loves to reference when he can.
